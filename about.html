SimpleMMO browser extension

About Extension

It is a Chrome extension for browser version of SimpleMMO game.

Created mainly for players that play on android devices with version below 5.0.

Author: Y0mu

Discord: Y0mu#0703

Email: justy0mu@gmail.com

About Game

SimpleMMO - The Lightweight MMO

SimpleMMO is a incremental massive multiplayer online role playing game that aims for simplicity. All you need to do is take a step with a simple click of a button and away you go! Traverse the world with strange, funny, serious, and downright ridiculous adventure texts with every step that you take.

Developed by: Dawsn Interactive Studios
